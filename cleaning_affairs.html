About Us

At Cleaning Affairs, we believe that cleanliness is not just about appearance—it’s about creating a healthier and more comfortable environment. Our mission is to redefine cleaning standards, offering services that not only meet but exceed client expectations.

Our Services

Post-Construction Cleaning

Deep Cleaning

Office Cleaning
